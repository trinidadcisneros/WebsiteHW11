The impact of Latitude on Max Temperature

Outcome: Temperature increases as the latitude for each city approaches "0".



Discussion:

The scatter plot reporesents 500 stochastically selected cities from -60 Lat (southern hemisphere) to ~ 90 Lat (northern hemisphere). The shape of the curve has an inverse parabaloid shape, with a "vertex" approximatly at the equator (0 latitude) +- 20 Latitude. The temperature than deops to < 0 degreed F at cities between 40-90 latitude. The shape of this curve confirms that as cities approach the equator, the temperature increases, with the max temp centered on the equator.
